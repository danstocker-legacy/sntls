Class: Hash

sntls. Hash

General wrapper around objects to treat them as hash. No `Object`-delegated methods on `.items` should be called as they may break code. All methods that operate *on* `.items` should be implemented on `Hash`. Other `Hash`-based classes may delegate conversion methods to this class.
