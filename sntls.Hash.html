Class: Hash

sntls. Hash

General wrapper around objects to treat them as hash. Calling `Object.prototype` methods on hash objects is not viable as they may be shadowed by user data, and such cases certainly lead the application to break. Other `Hash`-based classes may delegate conversion methods to this class.

items :Object

Object buffer that stores items. Technically writable and public for performance and transparency reasons, but should not be changed externally as may lead to inconsistent state especially in `Hash`-based subclasses.
